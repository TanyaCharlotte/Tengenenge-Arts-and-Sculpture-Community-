Tengenenge Arts and Sculptures Village is a renowned art community in Zimbabwe,dedicated to preserving and showcasing the rich tradition of Shona Stone sculpting and Art making.
Founded in the 1960s ,it has become a hub for most talented artists who transform raw stone into breathtaking sculptures and arts that reflect Zimabwean culture heritage and contemporary themes.
